# Dental-Implants-Market-Analysis-PowerBI
Clinical, Business &amp; Market Analysis of the global dental implant industry across 9 countries, 5 brands and 3 materials (2023–2024) | Power BI · DAX · Python

🦷**Dental Implants | Clinical, Business & Market Analysis
Power BI | DAX | Python | Data Storytelling**
**Overview**

This project goes beyond visualization — it delivers a comprehensive clinical, business and market analysis of the global dental implant industry across 9 countries, 3 continents, and 5 major brands for the period 2023–2024.
Built to answer one critical question:

"Which dental implant brand and material offers the best clinical outcome per dollar invested — and where are the biggest market opportunities?"


🔬**Three Layers of Analysis**

LayerFocusKey QuestionsClinicalSuccess rates, complication rates by brand & materialWhich implant is safest?BusinessPricing strategy, market share, regional positioningWhere is the best value?MarketGrowth opportunities by continent, brand penetrationWhere should we expand?

📊**Dashboard Pages**

PageDescriptionOverviewExecutive KPIs, market leaders by continent, price comparisonDetailed AnalysisSuccess rates, complication rates, patient satisfaction by brand and materialNarrative & InsightsSCQA business narrative and executive recommendations

💡**Key Findings**

**Straumann** leads globally with a **98.2% success rate**.
**Titanium Grade 5 outperforms Zirconia** clinically despite lower price.
**Latin America shows the strongest growth potential due to price sensitivity**.
**BioHorizons** offers the best value for **budget-conscious patients**.
**Zirconia** commands a $350 USD premium but **shows higher complication rates**.


📐**KPIs Tracked**

Total Units Sold
Average Price (USD)
Success Rate (%)
Complication Rate (%)
Patient Satisfaction Score (1–10)


🗂️**Dataset**

Field, Description, Brand, Material Titanium Grade 4 / Titanium Grade 5 / Zirconia, Country (9 countries), Continents, Latin America / Europe / North America, Price Average,  Price per implant, Clinical success rate (%), Complication rate (%), Patient satisfaction score (1–10), Units sold. 

Dataset built with real clinical benchmarks from dental literature combined with simulated commercial data for regional pricing and volume.
